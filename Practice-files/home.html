Code Queen is a women-focused digital skills training program primarily active in Uganda/East Africa, aimed at equipping young women with tech and coding skills so they can enter the tech workforce, start careers in software, or launch tech-driven businesses.

key Points about this Program:

  • It’s run by Educating The Children (ETC), a UK-registered education charity that focuses on creating opportunities for girls and young women.
  • The program provides training in software development, digital skills, work readiness, and mindset to help young women break into the tech industry.
  • Over the years, hundreds of women have graduated and gone on to jobs, internships, or entrepreneurship in tech.
  • There have been partnerships with organizations like the National ICT Innovation Hub in Kampala, expanding access to free quality training.
  • Code Queen celebrates its impact — e.g., events like “Code Queen @ 5” marked five years of empowering women through tech education.

This initiative is especially notable in Uganda and East Africa for tackling gender gaps in tech and encouraging more women to build sustainable careers in ICT.
